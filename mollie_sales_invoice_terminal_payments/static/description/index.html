Pay Sales orders & Invoices seamlessly via Mollie Terminal

Accept sales orders and invoice payments with the Mollie Terminal for a seamless payment experience.

Key Features

Take card, QR code, and tap-and-go payments
Plug and play - no set up required
Offer global and local payment methods

How to configure

Step 1 Download the module and place the module in apps folder.

Step 2 Install this module in your Odoo instance.

Step 3 Open Sales > Configuration > Payment Providers and open mollie provider.

Step 4 Click on the Mollie Terminal tab. And then click on View terminals .

Step 5 Click on Sync terminal to sync terminals from your account.

DONE That's it you are ready. You will see the mollie terminal payment buttons on sale order and invoices records.
